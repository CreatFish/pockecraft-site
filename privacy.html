口袋工坊（"我们"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私协议说明了我们在您使用 iOS 应用程序时如何处理信息。 Pockecraft ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

信息收集 Information We Collect

我们不收集任何个人信息。 We do not collect any personal information.

口袋工坊以隐私为核心设计原则： Pockecraft is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • 您创建的所有工具仅存储在您的设备本地 All tools you create are stored locally on your device only
  • 工具生成的所有数据均保留在您的设备上 All data generated by your tools remains on your device
  • 我们无法访问您的工具或数据 We do not have access to your tools or data
  • 我们不追踪您的使用行为 We do not track your usage or behavior
  • 我们不使用任何分析或追踪服务 We do not use analytics or tracking services

AI 服务 AI Service

当您创建或修改工具时，您的描述会通过我们的服务器发送至第三方 AI 服务来生成工具代码。我们可能使用不同的 AI 服务提供商（如智谱 GLM、Moonshot Kimi 等）以提供最佳服务。此通信： When you create or modify a tool, your description is sent through our server to third-party AI services to generate the tool code. We may use different AI service providers (such as Zhipu GLM, Moonshot Kimi, etc.) to deliver the best experience. This communication:

  • 仅包含您提供的工具描述和设备屏幕尺寸信息 Only includes the description you provide and device screen dimensions
  • 不包含任何个人信息 Does not include any personal information
  • 不包含您其他工具的数据 Does not include data from your other tools
  • 通过我们的安全服务器中转处理，您的描述不会被存储 Is relayed through our secure server and your descriptions are not stored

订阅数据 Subscription Data

如果您订阅了口袋工坊 Pro，订阅完全由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。我们无法访问您的支付信息。Apple 对您数据的处理受 Apple 隐私政策的约束。 If you subscribe to Pockecraft Pro, the subscription is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

数据存储 Data Storage

所有应用数据使用 iOS 原生文件系统存储在您的设备本地，包括： All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS's native file system. This includes:

  • 您创建的工具 Tools you create
  • 工具的配置和设置 Tool configurations and settings
  • 工具内生成的数据 Data generated within tools

如果您启用了 iCloud 备份，这些数据会包含在备份中，但我们无法访问这些备份。 This data is included in your iCloud backup if you have iCloud backup enabled for your device, but we do not have access to these backups.

数据删除 Data Deletion

您可以随时通过以下方式删除数据： You can delete your data at any time by:

  • 在应用内删除单个工具 Deleting individual tools within the app
  • 卸载应用（将删除所有应用数据） Uninstalling the app (removes all app data)

儿童隐私 Children's Privacy
