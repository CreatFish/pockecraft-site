Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Pockecraft ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Pockecraft is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • All tools you create are stored locally on your device only
  • All data generated by your tools remains on your device
  • We do not have access to your tools or data
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services

AI Service

When you create or modify a tool, your description is sent to a third-party AI service (DeepSeek) to generate the tool code. This communication:

  • Only includes the description you provide for tool generation
  • Does not include any personal information
  • Does not include data from your other tools
  • Is processed according to DeepSeek's Privacy Policy

Subscription Data

If you subscribe to Pockecraft Pro, the subscription is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS's native file system. This includes:

  • Tools you create
  • Tool configurations and settings
  • Data generated within tools

This data is included in your iCloud backup if you have iCloud backup enabled for your device, but we do not have access to these backups.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual tools within the app
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all app data)

Children's Privacy

Pockecraft does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require account creation or personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
