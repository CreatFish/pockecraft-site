Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a tool?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, describe what you want in natural language, and tap "Forge". The AI will generate a fully functional tool for you.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All your tools and data are stored locally on your device. We don't have access to your data, and nothing is uploaded to the cloud.

What's included in Pockecraft Pro?

Pockecraft Pro gives you unlimited tool creation, the ability to modify existing tools, and access to all future premium features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad.

Can I export my tools?

Tools are stored as HTML files on your device. You can access them through the app's share functionality.

Why isn't my tool working correctly?

AI-generated tools may occasionally have issues. Try using the "Reforge" feature to regenerate the tool with a more detailed description, or contact us for help.
